# Seventh Sanctum

The story is about a peasant who is constantly opposing a greedy champion. It takes place in a psychologist's office in Tokyo. The story begins with treason. Magic is fading in power, and that plays an important role in the story.
This is a coming-of-age story. The story is about an unhealthy barbarian who must work with a doctor. It starts in a zoo. The story ends with a theft. The question of when a machine becomes human is a major part of the story.
